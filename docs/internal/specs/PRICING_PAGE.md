## FAQ Section Headline

**Questions You Should Be Asking**

---

### 1. When do I need OMEGA?

If your product includes AI-driven decisions, automated actions, or user-facing AI features, you’ll eventually need production-grade execution infrastructure.

If you’re experimenting — ForgePilot is enough.

If real users depend on your AI — OMEGA becomes critical.

---

### 2. Do I need governance at launch?

Not always.

But once your product:

* Handles user data
* Automates decisions
* Executes account actions
* Accepts payments
* Serves multiple tenants

Governance stops being theoretical and becomes operational.

OMEGA includes governance automatically through Keon.

---

### 3. Can I use Keon without OMEGA?

Yes.

Keon can integrate with custom-built execution systems or existing AI infrastructure.

This is typically relevant for enterprise teams with in-house platforms.

---

### 4. Can I use OMEGA without Keon?

No.

OMEGA runs within Keon’s governance boundaries by design.

AI execution without governance creates avoidable risk. We don’t separate them.

---

### 5. What’s the difference between ForgePilot and OMEGA?

ForgePilot helps you design and plan your product.

OMEGA runs your AI infrastructure once you ship.

Think:

Blueprint → Execution Engine

---

### 6. Is ForgePilot just another AI chatbot?

No.

ForgePilot produces structured launch blueprints, revenue models, and technical architecture guidance.

It is built to function as an AI co-founder — not a conversational toy.

---

### 7. Can I upgrade later?

Yes.

Many founders begin with ForgePilot and adopt OMEGA once they launch AI-powered features.

Upgrade credits may apply based on plan duration.

---

### 8. Do you offer enterprise pricing?

Yes.

Keon standalone and high-scale OMEGA deployments are priced based on usage, deployment model, and compliance requirements.

Contact us for details.
