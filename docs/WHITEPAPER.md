# ForgePilot — Governed by OMEGA

ForgePilot is an AI-driven business validation and launch automation platform built on [OMEGA Governed Execution](https://keon.systems/whitepapers/omega-governed-execution).

## Governance Model

ForgePilot implements [Cryptographically Governed AI Execution (CGAE)](https://keon.systems/whitepapers/cgae) through the OMEGA substrate, ensuring:

- **Pre-Execution Evaluation** - Every AI action is evaluated against policy before execution
- **Cryptographic Binding** - Decisions are cryptographically bound to their governing policies
- **Verifiable Evidence** - Every action produces immutable, auditable evidence
- **Fail-Closed Enforcement** - Governance violations halt execution immediately
